What we do

We mainly work in three areas considering the compliance and ethics of using healthcare data.
  • Collect Healthcare Data
  • Analyze Data
  • Design Data-Driven Systems
We collect healthcare data from a variety of outlets. The data outlets might be surveys, administrative and medical records, claims data, vital records, surveillance, disease registries, and peer-reviewed literature.
We carry out all the necessary data cleaning (wrangling) and labeling/annotating processes to make the healthcare data suitable for smart system design and characterize and summarize basic features of the data using tabular and graphic visualizations.
Using the cleaned and labeled/annotated healthcare data, we design smart systems that can predict the anticipated next outcome, classify or detect abnormality and segment a given data into groups.
